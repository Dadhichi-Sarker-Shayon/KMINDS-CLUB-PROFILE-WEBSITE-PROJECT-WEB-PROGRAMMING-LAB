About K-MiNDS

Building the Next Generation of AI Engineers

K-MiNDS (Machine Intelligence and Data Science) is a student community at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), dedicated to fostering a strong AI/ML culture among students.

We offer structured learning paths from foundational machine learning to cutting-edge generative AI, computer vision, and natural language processing — all grounded in real-world projects and peer collaboration.

Our community bridges the gap between academic theory and industry practice, preparing KUET students for careers in AI research and engineering.

Learning Tracks

Course Milestones

Four structured tracks from fundamentals to frontier AI — each with projects, peer review, and mentorship.

🤖
Machine Learning
From Data to Decisions
Level 1: Noob's Guide — Linear Regression, Metrics, Heart Disease Prediction
Level 2: Core ML — KNN, Trees, Ensembles, Customer Segmentation
Level 3: DL Foundations — PyTorch, MNIST, Neural Network from Scratch
Supervised Learning Unsupervised Learning Feature Engineering PyTorch Backprop
🧠
Deep Learning
Neural Networks to GenAI
Level 1: Foundations — CNNs, ResNet, Transfer Learning
Level 2: Sequence Models — RNNs, LSTMs, Seq2Seq, Neural MT
Level 3: Generative AI — GANs, Diffusion Models, LLMs
CNNs RNNs/LSTMs GANs Diffusion Multimodal
👁️
Computer Vision
Pixels to Perception
Part 1: Vision Foundations — Convolutions, Segmentation, YOLO
Part 2: Modern Architectures — ViT, CLIP, BLIP, Hybrid Models
Part 3: Generative & 3D — NeRF, Gaussian Splatting, Optical Flow

Natural Language Processing
Text to Intelligence
Level 1: The Basics — Preprocessing, TF-IDF, Sentiment Analysis
