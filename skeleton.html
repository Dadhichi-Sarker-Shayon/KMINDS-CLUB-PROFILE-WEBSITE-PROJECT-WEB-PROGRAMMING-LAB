About K-MiNDS

Building the Next Generation of AI Engineers

K-MiNDS (Machine Intelligence and Data Science) is a student community at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), dedicated to fostering a strong AI/ML culture among students.

We offer structured learning paths from foundational machine learning to cutting-edge generative AI, computer vision, and natural language processing — all grounded in real-world projects and peer collaboration.

Our community bridges the gap between academic theory and industry practice, preparing KUET students for careers in AI research and engineering.
